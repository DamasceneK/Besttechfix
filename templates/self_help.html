Self-Help

Try these quick steps before booking a service appointment.

Slow Computer

  • Restart the computer.
  • Check storage space.
  • Remove apps you do not use.
  • Disable unnecessary startup apps.
  • Run Windows updates.

Printer Not Working

  • Restart the printer and computer.
  • Check if the printer is connected to Wi-Fi.
  • Make sure paper and toner/ink are loaded.
  • Remove and re-add the printer in Windows settings.

Phone Storage Full

  • Delete old videos and downloads.
  • Back up photos to iCloud or Google Photos.
  • Remove apps you do not use.
  • Clear large message attachments.
